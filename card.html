Hedgehog

This animal is immune to most adder's venom, making it easy prey for the hedgehog.

  • Scientific Name: Erinaceinae
  • Average Weight: 1 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 2-5 Years
  • Habitat: Woodlands and Meadows

The hedgehog is a carniverous animal that originates from Europe, Asia, and Africa. They have excellent hearing and sense of smell but weak eyesight. The hedgehog hunts with its sense of smell. The hedgehog mainly eats insects, snails, worms, mice, frogs and even snakes. The collective noun for a hedgehog is "an array of hedgehogs".
